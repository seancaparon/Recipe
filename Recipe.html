Bioled Egg Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place the eggs in a saucepan and add enough water to cover them by about an inch.
  2. Place the saucepan over medium-high heat and bring the water to a boil.
  3. Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to low and simmer the eggs for 9-12 minutes, depending on your desired level of doneness.
  4. Remove the eggs from the saucepan and place them in a bowl of cold water to cool for a few minutes.
  5. Peel the eggs and serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for later use.
